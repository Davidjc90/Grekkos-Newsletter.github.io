FIT FOR SUMMER

3 DAY PROGRAM
WITH COACH PAUL

Coach Paul knows the most efficient way to get lean for summer. With compound movements and full body work, he'll help you reach your beach body goals.

INTENTIONAL BREATHING

320 MINUTES
WITH COACH JARED

Maximal Expansive Breathing is a diaphragmic technique which provides centering and empowerment after a workout. Coach Jared shows you how to take your mind and body to a calm, improved state. Slow down, release tension, and enjoy the practice.

FIT FOR ALL BODIES

30 MINUTES
WITH COACH NATALIE

Strong people come in all sizes. This workout for large bodies was designed by Coach Natalie using feedback from actual members. With no "up and down" exercises and plenty of modifications, you can feel confident as you begin your strength journey.
